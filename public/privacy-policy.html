Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Invepin collects the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and organization details
  • Business Data: Inventory levels, sales data, employee information
  • Device Information: Camera access for barcode scanning and face recognition
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide bar management and inventory tracking services
  • Enable face recognition for staff clock-in/out
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate important updates and support

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Organization-level data isolation

4. Camera and Face Recognition

Our app uses camera access for:

  • Barcode scanning for inventory management
  • Face recognition for staff authentication
  • All biometric data is processed locally and encrypted
  • Face data is used only for authentication and is not shared

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your business data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
